    Contributing to My Attend¶

    We appreciate your interest in helping improve My Attend! Whether you're reporting bugs, suggesting features, or providing feedback, your contributions make our app better for everyone.

    Ways to Contribute¶

    üêõ Reporting Bugs¶

    Found a bug? Help us fix it by providing detailed information.

    Before Reporting a Bug¶

    1. Check if it's already reported - Search existing issues first
    2. Try basic troubleshooting - Restart the app, check for updates
    3. Reproduce the issue - Make sure it happens consistently
    4. Gather information - Screenshots, device details, steps to reproduce

    How to Report Bugs¶

    Email Method: Send detailed bug reports to bugs@myattend.com

    In-App Method: 1. Go to Profile > Help & Support 2. Tap "Report a Bug" 3. Fill out the bug report form 4. Submit with screenshots if applicable

    What to Include in Bug Reports¶

    Required Information: - App version (found in Profile > About) - Device information (iPhone 12, Samsung Galaxy S21, etc.) - Operating system version (iOS 15.2, Android 12, etc.) - Steps to reproduce the bug - Expected behavior vs. actual behavior - Screenshots or videos if helpful

    Example Bug Report: 

    Subject: Check-in button not responding on Android

App Version: 1.2.1
Device: Samsung Galaxy S22
OS: Android 13
Organization: ABC Company

Steps to reproduce:
1. Open My Attend app
2. Tap the "Check In" button
3. Button appears pressed but nothing happens
4. No check-in is recorded

Expected: Check-in should be recorded with timestamp
Actual: Button press has no effect

Additional info: This started happening after the latest app update.
Happens every time I try to check in.

    üí° Feature Requests¶

    Have an idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear it!

    How to Suggest Features¶

    Email Method: Send feature suggestions to feedback@myattend.com

    In-App Method: 1. Go to Profile > Help & Support 2. Tap "Suggest a Feature" 3. Describe your idea in detail 4. Submit your suggestion

    What Makes a Good Feature Request¶

    Include These Details: - Clear description of the proposed feature - Use case - Why would this be helpful? - Target users - Who would benefit from this feature? - Priority level - How important is this to you? - Alternative solutions - What do you currently do instead?

    Example Feature Request: 

    Subject: Bulk check-in for team events

Description: 
Add ability for managers to check in multiple employees at once 
for team events, meetings, or training sessions.

Use Case:
When our team has off-site training, it's tedious for everyone 
to individually check in. A bulk check-in would save time and 
ensure everyone's attendance is recorded.

Target Users:
Managers and team leads who organize group activities

Priority: Medium
Currently we manually adjust attendance records after events.

Suggested Implementation:
- "Bulk Check-in" option in admin panel
- Select multiple employees from list
- Set common check-in time
- Add notes about the event

    üìù Documentation Feedback¶

    Help us improve our documentation and user guides.

    Types of Documentation Feedback¶

    • Unclear instructions - Steps that are confusing or incomplete
    • Missing information - Topics that should be covered but aren't
    • Outdated content - Information that's no longer accurate
    • Typos and errors - Grammar, spelling, or factual mistakes
    • Suggestions for improvement - Better ways to explain concepts

    How to Provide Documentation Feedback¶

    Send feedback to docs@myattend.com with:

    • Page or section you're commenting on
    • Specific issue you encountered
    • Suggested improvement if you have one
    • Your user type (employee, admin, new user, etc.)

    üé® User Experience Feedback¶

    Share your experience using My Attend to help us improve usability.

    What We're Looking For¶

    • Workflow pain points - Tasks that are difficult or confusing
    • Interface suggestions - Better ways to organize or display information
    • Accessibility issues - Problems with screen readers, color contrast, etc.
    • Performance feedback - Slow loading, crashes, or other technical issues
    • Mobile experience - Issues specific to phone or tablet usage

    How to Share UX Feedback¶

    Email ux@myattend.com with:

    • Your role (employee, manager, admin)
    • Specific task you were trying to accomplish
    • What went well and what was frustrating
    • Suggestions for improvement
    • Screenshots if helpful

    Beta Testing Program¶

    Get early access to new features and help shape the future of My Attend.

    Beta Program Benefits¶

    • Early access to new features (2-4 weeks before general release)
    • Direct feedback channel to our development team
    • Influence product direction with your input
    • Priority support for beta-related issues
    • Exclusive updates on development progress

    How to Join the Beta Program¶

    1. Email beta@myattend.com with "Beta Program" in the subject
    2. Include your information:
    3. Organization name
    4. Your role (admin, manager, employee)
    5. Current app version you're using
    6. Device type and OS version
    7. We'll respond within 48 hours with beta access instructions
    8. Install the beta version following our instructions
    9. Start testing and providing feedback

    Beta Testing Guidelines¶

    What We Expect: - Regular usage of beta features in your daily workflow - Timely feedback on bugs and usability issues - Participation in beta surveys and feedback sessions - Patience with beta bugs and incomplete features

    What You Can Expect: - Weekly beta updates with new features and fixes - Direct communication with our development team - Advance notice of major changes or new features - Recognition in release notes for significant contributions

    Community Guidelines¶

    Help us maintain a positive and productive community.

    Our Values¶

    • Respectful communication - Treat everyone with courtesy and professionalism
    • Constructive feedback - Focus on solutions and improvements
    • Collaborative spirit - Work together to make My Attend better
    • Inclusive environment - Welcome users of all backgrounds and skill levels

    Communication Standards¶

    Do: - Be specific and detailed in your feedback - Provide context for your suggestions - Acknowledge others' contributions - Stay focused on improving the product

    Don't: - Use offensive or inappropriate language - Make personal attacks or criticisms - Share confidential company information - Spam or repeatedly submit the same feedback

    Recognition and Rewards¶

    We appreciate our contributors and like to show our gratitude.

    How We Recognize Contributors¶

    Public Recognition: - Release notes mention significant contributors - Social media shout-outs for great feedback - Community spotlight in our newsletter - Beta tester credits in the app

    Exclusive Benefits: - Early access to new features - Direct line to our development team - Influence on product roadmap decisions - Special badges in your app profile (coming soon)

    Contributor Levels¶

    Community Member - Anyone who provides feedback or reports bugs Active Contributor - Regular feedback providers and beta testers Community Champion - Top contributors who help shape product direction Advisory Panel - Select contributors who participate in product planning

    Development Roadmap¶

    Stay informed about what's coming next in My Attend.

    Current Focus Areas¶

    Q1 2026: - Apple Watch integration - Check-in from your wrist - Advanced analytics - Predictive insights and trends - Improved offline mode - More features available without internet - Enhanced security - Additional authentication options

    Q2 2026: - Slack/Teams integration - Attendance notifications in chat - Custom fields - Organization-specific data collection - Advanced scheduling - Shift planning and management - Mobile kiosk mode - Shared device check-in stations

    How Feedback Influences Development¶

    Your contributions directly impact our roadmap:

    • Popular feature requests get prioritized for development
    • Critical bugs receive immediate attention
    • Usability feedback drives interface improvements
    • Integration requests influence our partnership strategy

    Privacy and Data¶

    Feedback Privacy¶

    When you provide feedback:

    • Personal information is kept confidential
    • Feedback content may be shared internally for product improvement
    • Contact details are used only for follow-up communication
    • Data retention follows our standard privacy policy

    What We Don't Share¶

    • Your personal information with third parties
    • Specific feedback publicly without permission
    • Company-specific details from your organization
    • Beta features or unreleased information
