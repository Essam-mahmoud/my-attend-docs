    User Guide¶

    Learn the basics of using My Attend effectively. This guide covers essential features that both employees and administrators need to know.

    Getting Started¶

    Logging In¶

    My Attend supports different user roles with tailored interfaces:

    Employee Login¶

    1. Open My Attend on your device
    2. Enter your credentials:
    3. Email address or employee ID
    4. Password
    5. Tap "Sign In"
    6. You'll see the employee dashboard with check-in options

    Admin Login¶

    1. Open My Attend on your device
    2. Enter your admin credentials:
    3. Admin email address
    4. Password
    5. Tap "Sign In"
    6. You'll see the admin dashboard with management tools

    Password Reset¶

    Forgot your password? No problem:

    1. Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Check your email for reset instructions
    4. Follow the link to create a new password
    5. Log in with your new password

    Dashboard Overview¶

    Employee Dashboard¶

    When you log in as an employee, you'll see:

    • Quick Check-in/Check-out button - Large, prominent button for attendance
    • Today's status - Current work hours and break time
    • Recent activity - Your last few check-ins and check-outs
    • Upcoming schedule - Your work schedule for the week
    • Quick actions - Request leave, view reports, update profile

    Admin Dashboard¶

    Administrators see a comprehensive overview:

    • Team status - Who's currently checked in/out
    • Today's summary - Attendance statistics for today
    • Pending approvals - Leave requests awaiting your decision
    • Recent activity - Latest team check-ins and activities
    • Quick actions - Add employees, generate reports, manage settings

    Basic Operations¶

    Checking In and Out¶

    The core feature of My Attend - tracking your work hours:

    Quick Check-in¶

    1. Open the app when you arrive at work
    2. Tap the large "Check In" button on your dashboard
    3. Confirm your location if prompted
    4. See confirmation that you're checked in
    5. Your status updates to "Checked In"

    Quick Check-out¶

    1. Open the app when leaving work
    2. Tap the "Check Out" button (now visible)
    3. Confirm the action if prompted
    4. See your total hours for the day
    5. Your status updates to "Checked Out"

    Viewing Your Attendance¶

    Keep track of your work hours and attendance history:

    1. Go to Reports section from bottom navigation
    2. Select date range you want to view
    3. See detailed breakdown:
    4. Check-in and check-out times
    5. Total hours worked
    6. Break times taken
    7. Overtime hours
    8. Export reports if needed for your records

    Managing Your Profile¶

    Keep your information up to date:

    1. Tap Profile in bottom navigation
    2. Update your details:
    3. Profile photo
    4. Contact information
    5. Emergency contacts
    6. Notification preferences
    7. Save changes when done

    Notifications¶

    My Attend keeps you informed with helpful notifications:

    Types of Notifications¶

    • Check-in reminders - When your shift starts
    • Check-out reminders - When your shift ends
    • Break reminders - Time for scheduled breaks
    • Schedule updates - Changes to your work schedule
    • Leave approvals - Status of your time-off requests

    Managing Notifications¶

    Customize what notifications you receive:

    1. Go to Profile > Settings
    2. Tap "Notifications"
    3. Toggle on/off different notification types
    4. Set quiet hours if needed
    5. Choose notification sounds

    Offline Mode¶

    My Attend works even when you don't have internet:

    What Works Offline¶

    • Check-in and check-out - Actions are saved locally
    • View recent attendance - Last synced data remains available
    • Basic profile information - Your details stay accessible

    When You're Back Online¶

    • Automatic sync - All offline actions upload automatically
    • Data updates - Latest information downloads
    • Notifications catch up - Any missed alerts appear

    Common Tasks¶

    Requesting Time Off¶

    Need a day off? Submit a request easily:

    1. Go to Schedule section
    2. Tap "Request Leave"
    3. Select dates you need off
    4. Choose leave type:
    5. Vacation
    6. Sick leave
    7. Personal day
    8. Other
    9. Add a note explaining your request
    10. Submit for approval
    11. Get notified when approved or denied

    Viewing Team Status (Admins)¶

    See who's currently working:

    1. Check the admin dashboard for real-time status
    2. View "Team Status" section

    Generating Reports (Admins)¶

    Create attendance reports for your team:

    1. Go to Reports section
    2. Select report type:
    3. Daily summary
    4. Weekly overview
    5. Monthly report
    6. Custom date range
    7. Choose employees to include
    8. Generate report
    9. Export as PDF or Excel if needed

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues¶

    Can't check in - Ensure you're at the correct location - Check your internet connection - Try restarting the app

    Missing check-in/out - Contact your administrator to add manual entry - Check if you were in offline mode - Verify your location settings

    Notifications not working - Check notification permissions in device settings - Ensure notifications are enabled in app settings - Try logging out and back in

    App running slowly - Close other apps to free up memory - Restart your device - Update to the latest app version

    Getting Help¶

    When you need assistance:

    1. Check the FAQ - Common questions and answers
    2. Use in-app help - Tap the help icon in any section
    3. Contact support - Email support@myattend.com
    4. Ask your admin - They may have organization-specific guidance

    Tips for Success¶

    For Employees¶

    • Check in promptly when you arrive at work
    • Keep the app updated for the best experience
    • Review your attendance regularly to catch any issues
    • Submit leave requests early to ensure approval

    For Administrators¶

    • Set clear policies about attendance expectations
    • Review reports regularly to identify patterns
    • Respond to leave requests promptly to keep employees informed
    • Keep employee information updated for accurate reporting
