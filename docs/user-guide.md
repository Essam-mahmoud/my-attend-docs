# User Guide

Learn the basics of using My Attend effectively. This guide covers essential features that both employees and administrators need to know.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

My Attend supports different user roles with tailored interfaces:

#### Employee Login
1. **Open My Attend** on your device
2. **Enter your credentials**:
   - Email address or employee ID
   - Password
3. **Tap "Sign In"**
4. **You'll see the employee dashboard** with check-in options

#### Admin Login
1. **Open My Attend** on your device
2. **Enter your admin credentials**:
   - Admin email address
   - Password
3. **Tap "Sign In"**
4. **You'll see the admin dashboard** with management tools

### Password Reset

Forgot your password? No problem:

1. **Tap "Forgot Password?"** on the login screen
2. **Enter your email address**
3. **Check your email** for reset instructions
4. **Follow the link** to create a new password
5. **Log in** with your new password

## Basic Operations

### Checking In and Out

The core feature of My Attend - tracking your work hours:

#### Quick Check-in
1. **Open the app** when you arrive at work
2. **Tap the large "Check In" button** on your dashboard
3. **Confirm your location** if prompted
4. **See confirmation** that you're checked in
5. **Your status updates** to "Checked In"

#### Quick Check-out
1. **Open the app** when leaving work
2. **Tap the "Check Out" button** (now visible)
3. **Confirm the action** if prompted
4. **See your total hours** for the day
5. **Your status updates** to "Checked Out"

### Viewing Your Attendance

Keep track of your work hours and attendance history:

1. **Go to Reports section** from bottom navigation
2. **Select date range** you want to view
3. **See detailed breakdown**:
   - Check-in and check-out times
   - Total hours worked
   - Break times taken
   - Overtime hours
4. **Export reports** if needed for your records

### Managing Your Profile

Keep your information up to date:

1. **Tap Profile** in bottom navigation
2. **Update your details**:
   - Profile photo
   - Contact information
   - Emergency contacts
   - Notification preferences
3. **Save changes** when done

## Notifications

My Attend keeps you informed with helpful notifications:

### Types of Notifications

- **Check-in reminders** - When your shift starts
- **Check-out reminders** - When your shift ends
- **Break reminders** - Time for scheduled breaks
- **Schedule updates** - Changes to your work schedule
- **Leave approvals** - Status of your time-off requests

### Managing Notifications

Customize what notifications you receive:

1. **Go to Profile > Settings**
2. **Tap "Notifications"**
3. **Toggle on/off** different notification types
4. **Set quiet hours** if needed
5. **Choose notification sounds**

## Offline Mode

My Attend works even when you don't have internet:

### What Works Offline
- **Check-in and check-out** - Actions are saved locally
- **View recent attendance** - Last synced data remains available
- **Basic profile information** - Your details stay accessible

### When You're Back Online
- **Automatic sync** - All offline actions upload automatically
- **Data updates** - Latest information downloads
- **Notifications catch up** - Any missed alerts appear

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't check in**
- Ensure you're at the correct location
- Check your internet connection
- Try restarting the app

**Missing check-in/out**
- Contact your administrator to add manual entry
- Check if you were in offline mode
- Verify your location settings

**Notifications not working**
- Check notification permissions in device settings
- Ensure notifications are enabled in app settings
- Try logging out and back in

**App running slowly**
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Restart your device
- Update to the latest app version
