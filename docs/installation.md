# Installation Guide

Get My Attend installed on your device in just a few simple steps. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices.

## Download from App Stores

### iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1. **Open the App Store** on your iOS device
2. **Search for "My Attend"** in the search bar
3. **Tap "Get"** to download the app
4. **Wait for installation** to complete
5. **Open the app** from your home screen

[![Download on App Store](images/app-store-badge.png){ width="70" }](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/my-attend/id6754170055)

### Android

1. **Open Google Play Store** on your Android device
2. **Search for "My Attend"** in the search bar
3. **Tap "Install"** to download the app
4. **Wait for installation** to complete
5. **Open the app** from your app drawer

[![Get it on Google Play](images/google-play-badge.png){ width="70" }](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.smartnetworkai.iattend&hl=en)

## System Requirements

### iOS Requirements
- **Operating System**: iOS 15.0 or later
- **Compatible Devices**: iPhone 6s and newer, iPad Air 2 and newer
- **Storage Space**: 50MB available space
- **Internet**: Required for initial setup and data synchronization

### Android Requirements
- **Operating System**: Android 6.0 (API level 23) or later
- **RAM**: 2GB minimum, 4GB recommended
- **Storage Space**: 50MB available space
- **Internet**: Required for initial setup and data synchronization

## First-Time Setup

After installing the app, follow these steps to get started:

### 1. Create Your Account

1. **Open My Attend** on your device
2. **Tap "Sign Up"** if you're new to the app
3. **Enter your details**:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Create a secure password
4. **Verify your email** by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. **Complete your profile** with additional information

### 2. Join Your Organization

If your company is already using My Attend:

1. **Ask your admin** for the organization code
2. **Tap "Join Organization"** on the welcome screen
3. **Enter the organization code** provided by your admin
4. **Wait for approval** from your administrator
5. **Start using the app** once approved

### 3. Set Up Your Organization (Admins Only)

If you're setting up My Attend for your company:

1. **Tap "Create Organization"** on the welcome screen
2. **Enter company details**:
   - Company name
   - Industry type
   - Number of employees
   - Time zone
3. **Configure attendance settings**:
   - Work hours
   - Break times
   - Location requirements
4. **Add your first employees** or send invitation codes

## Permissions Setup

My Attend needs certain permissions to work properly:

### Location Permission
- **Why needed**: To verify employees are at the correct work location
- **When prompted**: Allow location access for accurate attendance tracking
- **Privacy**: Location data is only used during check-in/check-out

### Notification Permission
- **Why needed**: To send reminders and important updates
- **When prompted**: Allow notifications to stay informed
- **Control**: You can customize notification settings later

### Camera Permission (Optional)
- **Why needed**: For profile photos and document uploads
- **When prompted**: Allow camera access if you want to add photos
- **Optional**: You can skip this and add photos later

## Updating the App

Keep My Attend updated for the best experience and latest features:

### iOS Updates
1. **Open the App Store**
2. **Tap your profile icon** in the top right
3. **Scroll to "Available Updates"**
4. **Find My Attend** and tap "Update"
5. **Wait for update** to complete

### Android Updates
1. **Open Google Play Store**
2. **Tap the menu icon** (three lines)
3. **Select "My apps & games"**
4. **Find My Attend** in the updates list
5. **Tap "Update"**

### Auto-Updates
Enable automatic updates to always have the latest version:

- **iOS**: Settings > App Store > App Updates (toggle on)
- **Android**: Play Store > Settings > Auto-update apps

## Troubleshooting Installation

### Common Issues

**App won't download**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have enough storage space
- Try restarting your device

**Can't find the app in store**
- Make sure you're searching for "My Attend"
- Check if your device meets system requirements
- Try searching by developer name

**Installation fails**
- Clear your app store cache (Android)
- Sign out and back into your app store account
- Contact support if the problem persists
