# Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about My Attend. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

## Getting Started

### How do I download and install My Attend?

My Attend is available on both iOS and Android:

- **iOS**: Download from the App Store by searching "My Attend"
- **Android**: Download from Google Play Store by searching "My Attend"
- **System Requirements**: iOS 15+ or Android 8.0+

For detailed installation instructions, see our [Installation Guide](installation.md).

### How do I create an account?

There are two ways to get started:

1. **Join existing organization**: Get an organization code from your admin and use "Join Organization" option
2. **Create new organization**: Use "Create Organization" if you're setting up My Attend for your company

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. **Tap "Forgot Password?"** on the login screen
2. **Enter your email address**
3. **Check your email** for reset instructions
4. **Follow the link** to create a new password
5. **Log in** with your new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

### Can I use My Attend on multiple devices?

Yes! Your account syncs across all your devices. Simply log in with the same credentials on each device. Your attendance data will be automatically synchronized.

## Daily Usage

### How do I check in and out?

**Check-in:**
1. Open My Attend when you arrive at work
2. Tap the large "Check In" button
3. Confirm your location if prompted
4. See confirmation of your check-in time

**Check-out:**
1. Open My Attend when leaving work
2. Tap the "Check Out" button
3. Review your day's summary
4. Confirm the check-out

### What if I forget to check in or out?

Don't worry! Contact your supervisor or admin immediately:

- **Provide the correct times** you actually started/ended work
- **Explain the circumstances** (forgot phone, app issues, etc.)
- **Your admin can add manual entries** to correct your attendance

### Why does the app ask for my location?

Location verification ensures you're checking in from your designated work location. This helps prevent:

- **Remote check-ins** when you should be on-site
- **Buddy punching** (someone else checking you in)
- **Accurate location tracking** for multi-site businesses

Your location is only checked during check-in/out, not continuously tracked.

### Can I check in without internet?

Yes! My Attend works offline:

- **Check-in/out offline** - Actions are saved locally
- **Automatic sync** - Data uploads when internet returns
- **No data loss** - Everything is preserved safely

## Leave and Time Off

### How do I request time off?

1. **Go to Schedule** section in the app
2. **Tap "Request Leave"**
3. **Select your dates** and leave type
4. **Add a note** explaining your request (optional)
5. **Submit for approval**
6. **Get notified** when approved or denied

### How long does leave approval take?

Approval times depend on your organization's policy:

- **Typical response**: 24-48 hours for most requests
- **Urgent requests**: Contact your manager directly
- **Advance notice**: Submit requests as early as possible
- **Blackout periods**: Some dates may have restrictions

### How do I check my leave balance?

1. **Go to Profile** section
2. **Tap "Leave Balance"** or check your dashboard
3. **See current balances** for different leave types:
   - Vacation days
   - Sick leave
   - Personal days
   - Other leave types

### Can I cancel a leave request?

Yes, if it hasn't been approved yet:

1. **Go to Schedule > Leave Requests**
2. **Find your pending request**
3. **Tap "Cancel Request"**
4. **Confirm cancellation**

For approved requests, contact your manager to discuss changes.

## Technical Issues

### The app won't let me check in. What's wrong?

Common causes and solutions:

**Location issues:**
- Ensure GPS/location services are enabled
- Make sure you're within the allowed check-in area
- Try moving closer to your work location

**App issues:**
- Force close and reopen the app
- Check for app updates in your app store
- Restart your device

**Account issues:**
- Verify you're logged in with correct credentials
- Check if your account is active (contact admin)
- Ensure you're scheduled to work today

### Why aren't I receiving notifications?

Check these settings:

**Device settings:**
- Enable notifications for My Attend in device settings
- Check "Do Not Disturb" mode isn't blocking notifications
- Ensure notification sounds are enabled

**App settings:**
- Go to Profile > Settings > Notifications
- Enable the notification types you want
- Check notification timing preferences

**Account settings:**
- Verify your email address is correct
- Check spam folder for email notifications
- Contact admin if organization notifications are disabled

### The app is running slowly or crashing. How do I fix it?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

1. **Close other apps** to free up memory
2. **Restart your device** to clear temporary issues
3. **Update the app** to the latest version
4. **Clear app cache** (Android) or reinstall (iOS)
5. **Check available storage** - ensure you have enough space

If problems persist, contact support with your device model and app version.

### My attendance data looks wrong. What should I do?

**Check for common issues:**
- Verify you checked out at the end of each day
- Look for any missed check-ins or check-outs
- Check if break times are being calculated correctly
- Review any manual adjustments made by admins

**Contact your admin if:**
- Data is still incorrect after checking
- You need manual time adjustments
- There are system-wide issues affecting multiple employees

## Account and Security

### How do I change my password?

1. **Go to Profile > Settings**
2. **Tap "Security" or "Password"**
3. **Enter your current password**
4. **Enter your new password** (must meet requirements)
5. **Confirm new password**
6. **Save changes**

### Is my data secure?

Yes! My Attend uses enterprise-grade security:

- **Encrypted data** transmission and storage
- **Secure authentication** with strong password requirements
- **Regular security audits** and updates
- **GDPR compliance** for data protection
- **No tracking** outside of work-related activities

### Can I delete my account?

Account deletion must be handled by your organization's admin:

1. **Contact your admin** or HR department
2. **Request account deactivation** when you leave the company
3. **Data retention** follows your organization's policy
4. **Personal data** can be removed upon request

### How do I update my personal information?

1. **Go to Profile** section
2. **Tap "Edit Profile"**
3. **Update your information**:
   - Contact details
   - Emergency contacts
   - Profile photo
   - Personal preferences
4. **Save changes**

Some information (like job title or department) may need to be updated by your admin.

## Admin and Management

### How do I add new employees? (Admins)

1. **Go to Employees** section in admin panel
2. **Tap "Add Employee"**
3. **Fill in employee details**
4. **Set permissions** and role level
5. **Send invitation** or provide login credentials
6. **Employee completes** setup process

### How do I generate reports? (Admins)

1. **Go to Reports** section
2. **Select report type** (daily, weekly, monthly, custom)
3. **Choose date range** and employees
4. **Generate report**
5. **Export as PDF or Excel** if needed

### How do I approve leave requests? (Admins)

1. **Check Approvals** section on admin dashboard
2. **Review pending requests** with employee details
3. **Check leave balances** and team coverage
4. **Approve or deny** with optional notes
5. **Employee gets notified** automatically

### Can I set up multiple work locations?

Yes! Admins can configure multiple locations:

1. **Go to Settings > Locations**
2. **Add new location** with address and GPS coordinates
3. **Set geofencing radius** for each location
4. **Assign employees** to appropriate locations
5. **Configure location-specific** rules if needed
