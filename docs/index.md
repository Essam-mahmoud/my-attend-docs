# Welcome to My Attend

**Track employee attendance effortlessly with My Attend!**

My Attend is a powerful, user-friendly mobile app designed to streamline employee attendance management for businesses of all sizes. Whether you're a small team or a large organization, My Attend helps you track work hours, manage check-ins and check-outs, and generate comprehensive reports with ease.


## Why Choose My Attend?

### For Business Owners & Managers
- **Real-time tracking** - Monitor employee attendance instantly
- **Detailed reports** - Generate comprehensive attendance summaries
- **Easy approval system** - Handle leave requests with one tap
- **Cost-effective** - Reduce administrative overhead
- **Secure & reliable** - Your data is protected and always accessible

### For Employees
- **Simple check-in/out** - Clock in and out with just one tap
- **View your history** - Track your own attendance records
- **Request time off** - Submit vacation and leave requests easily
- **Stay informed** - Get notifications about schedule changes
